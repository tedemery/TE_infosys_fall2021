How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Get all the ingredients for sandwich.
  2. Place the 1st slice of bread down.
  3. Place your first ingredient
  4. Place the rest of your ingredients in the order you like.
  5. When done with ingredients, place the 2nd slice bread on top.
